Overview

The PowerPoint templates provide a short, brand-compliant company introduction deck. They follow the current Thinkport Corporate Design with official brand colors, typography and logos, and can be used as a starting point for internal and external presentations.

Content

  • Title slide with Thinkport logo and Venitus lockup
  • Slides for company overview, services and key facts
  • Layouts for text, bullet points and simple visuals
  • Consistent use of brand colors and Montserrat typography

Technical

  • Based on the latest Thinkport Slide Master guidelines
  • Colors from the official brand palette (Dark Blue, Orange, Turquoise)
  • Typography using Montserrat for headings and body text
  • Official logos from assets/logos/
